History

Panzer Kaserne and nearby Patch Barracks were constructed for the German Army and were joined by a specially designed trail due to the weight of tank traffic. There is also a small nearby training area- unusual for a Kaserne in an urban or suburban area.


The scout hut is currently located in an old German military building that is connected to the tower that German Army leaders used to observe tank movements
